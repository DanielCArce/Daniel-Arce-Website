# Case Study : FINA Mobile App
------
## Problem

The company growt to a level that don't handle correctly the orders from clients and some orders are
missing withn all the orders and overlap from the new ones or dont get correctly follow-up for check
if its are done or some items of thems are'nt complete.

Actually all the process are manage for books in papers and avoid a fast review about orders, items
and shipppings for clients and eventualy a follow-up of invoices and cash advances.

## Solution

A mobile app that help to company have a control about what order have shippings,
items pendings of ship and how much are waiting for receive, flexible for allow
recive orders in front of clients and share it with them inmediatily.

### Technology Stack
*Mobile App Stack*

- React@18.
- React Native.

*Backend Service API*

- NodeJs.
- ExpressJS.
- PrismaORM.
- PostgrelSQL.
